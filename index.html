About

I was born and raised in Detroit, MI. By my mother Deanna, and my father Richard. I grew up with one older brother, also named Richard, who is the person behind my love for computers and technology. Everything started in 6th grade. The first time I got a computer, which brought me to understand more about them. I was the kid in class that everyone knew about. It started with unblocking YouTube, to using inspect element to changes parts of a website. It wasn't until 8th grade I actually learned what inspect element, or Developer Tools, was for. Once I did, it made me go further into websites, how they're made, and what makes them run. This fascinated me so much that I have devoted the past 4 years of my life to Web Development and Design.
