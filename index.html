About

I'm Rayna, a skilled technician with a passion for technology and a talent for problem-solving. I've gained over four years of experience repairing tablets, phones, and computers, and building computers, which has helped me become an expert in my field. Additionally, I've honed my responsive web development skills over the years, creating websites using HTML, JS, CSS, and PHP.

In 2018, I participated in DHACK and won 2nd place in the Intermediate category, which was a great achievement for me. I've also gained entry-level experience in Python, and intermediate experience in KVM, maintaining, and diagnosing Linux distros.

I'm currently 21 years old and working as an SSA for Rivian, a company at the forefront of the electric vehicle industry. With my passion for technology and my impressive skill set, I'm determined to continue making waves in the tech industry for years to come.

You can explore my website to learn more about my background, browse my portfolio of projects, and get in touch with me to discuss how I can help you with your tech needs. Thank you for visiting!
